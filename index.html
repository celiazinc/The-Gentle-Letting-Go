Together with Leaf,
we can all contribute to saving our Earth

Why?

Transportation carbon emission caused 28% portion of greenhouse gas emissions in the U.S.   On the other hand, the United States is the global leader in food waste, with nearly 40 million tons of food every year.  Our earth is at a critical stage where everyone of us should contribute to protect our homeland.

How Can Leaf Help?

Carbon emission calculator can help you calculate your everyday carbon emission from transportation and household.

Our insight dashboard can help you track your carbon footprint and see your off-set progress.

Collecting Points through doing offset activities. You can use those point to plant a real tree in real life. Take actions to help our Earth!
